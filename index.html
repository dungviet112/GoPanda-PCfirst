TOP DESTINATIONS IN CHINA

Beijing

China's capital & home to major sites such as the Forbidden City palace complex & Tiananmen Square.

Xi'an

An ancient Chinese dynastic capital, Xi'an is home to Big Wild Goose Pagoda & the Terra Cotta Army.

Guilin

Southern Chinese town with hilly karst landscape, twin pagodas & boat tours on Cedar & Banyan lakes.

Guangzhou

Large Chinese city featuring modern architecture & colonial homes on Shamian Island.

Hangzhou

Chinese city famed for its expansive West Lake, its Grand Canal & nearby Longjin tea producers.

Zhangjiajie

Gateway to Wulingyuan Scenic Area, with 330m Bailong Elevator, Tianmen Mountain & cliff walkway.
